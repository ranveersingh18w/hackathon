Our Mission

To empower every internet user in India to fight misinformation with a powerful, easy-to-use tool powered by AI consensus.

Why We're Building This

The Problem: A Digital Wildfire

Fake news spreads on platforms like WhatsApp faster than anyone can control it. It causes real-world harm, and there's no fast, reliable, or easy-to-use "fire extinguisher" for the average person.

Our Mission: Empower Users

Our goal is to build that fire extinguisher. We want to give people a simple tool to check information instantly, right where they receive it. This isn't just about saying "true" or "false"‚Äîit's about building digital literacy and restoring trust.

The Plan: How It Works

Our Unique Idea: The "AI Council"

Instead of relying on one AI, we ask a "council" of different AIs (Gemini, GPT, etc.) for their opinion. This reduces bias and gives us a more reliable answer, like asking multiple experts.

How the System Connects

1. User's WhatsApp: Sends a message to our number.

↓

2. Python on Google Cloud: A small program receives the message.
