The Challenge: A Gap in Transparency

In a market flooded with choices, how can consumers be certain of the purity and origin of their Ayurvedic medicine? The journey from herb collection to finished product is complex and often opaque, creating a need for a reliable source of truth.

The 8-Step Journey We Track on the Blockchain

Ayurvedic medicine ki journey ka har important stage, khet se pharmacy tak, humare digital record mein a-mit aur verifiable block ke roop mein record kiya jaata hai.

1. Raw Herbs Collection

Sahi herb (jaise root, stem, leaf) ko certified jagah se laana. Blockchain par yeh pehli entry origin confirm karti hai.

2. Herbs ka Quality Check

Experts check karte hain ki herb asli hai, pure hai, aur usmein heavy metals ya pesticides toh nahi hain. Lab results ko as a record upload kiya jaata hai.

3. Processing & Purification

(Shodhana) Kuch khaas herbs ke liye yeh zaroori step hai. Isse natural toxicity nikalti hai aur medicinal properties behtar hoti hain.

4. Dawai Banana (Formulation)

(Nirmana) Yeh main manufacturing phase hai jahan herbs ko final form (powder, tablet, oil) mein banaya jaata hai. Batch ka formula record hota hai.

5. In-Process & Finished QC

Manufacturing ke dauraan aur baad mein samples test kiye jaate hain. Isse consistency, safety, aur microbial contamination check hota hai.

6. Standardization

Final product ko test karke ensure kiya jaata hai ki har batch mein active ingredients ki quantity same ho, jisse potency aayegi.

7. Packaging & Labeling

Medicine ko saaf containers mein pack kiya jaata hai. AYUSH-compliant label lagta hai. Ek unique QR code generate hota hai jo batch ki poori history se link hota hai.

8. Distribution & Market Check

Product ko market mein bheja jaata hai. Yeh final block distribution details aur post-market quality checks ki jaankari rakhta hai.

Har Upload Ka Pura Record

Jab bhi company 8 steps mein se koi bhi data upload karti hai, hum sirf process ka naam nahi, balki yeh saari details uske saath record karte hain. Yeh sab information ek permanent, secure block mein band ho jaati hai.

Secure Hash

Har entry ka unique digital fingerprint, jisse tampering na ho sake.

Weight / Quantity

Raw material ya product ka vajan aur quantity record hoti hai.

Timestamp

Data kab upload hua, uska exact time automatically record hota hai.

Geo-Location

Data kahan se upload hua, uski location GPS se track ho sakti hai.

Supporting Documents

Official lab reports, certificates, aur doosre proof (PDFs).

Photos & Videos

Real-time photos ya videos, jaise herbs ka stock ya packaging line.

Chain Link

How The Blockchain Works

Each block contains data (like a QC report), its own unique hash (a digital fingerprint), and the hash of the previous block. This link makes the chain incredibly secure. Changing a past block is impossible as it would break the entire chain.
